About the Gallery

What is the gallery?

This gallery was made to show off some of our members projects and artwork, and to show what Art for All is all about.



How is art chosen for the gallery?

All of the art chosen for this gallery is picked at random, and only with the artists permission.



How long will an art piece stay here for?

Art pieces will remain on this site indefinitely, but can always be removed on request by the artist.
